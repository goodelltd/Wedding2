Our Story

Vicki's point of view: After saying goodbyes, Vicki secured Tyler’s email address and the rest is history. Well, that and about 15 flights to and from Texas, with side trips to Chile, New York, and West Virginia. Plus about 800 phone calls and even more texts. Basically we couldn’t be happier, and we are so thankful to Tom and Kim especially, but everyone who played a part in getting us together.

Tyler's point of view: I was just visiting my close friends, Kim and TJ, when Kim brought up that she had a cousin who was single. She then showed me a picture of her and I said, "Why not? Midland is right next door. And blonds are the big leagues." Then, fearing a long-distance relationship, I had second thoughts.
The night before I was about to leave St. George, I spent the night playing cards with Vicki and her family, and she didn't mind that I was cheating. I thought, "This girl is pretty chill. I would totally date her if she didn't live a billion miles away." I then left town not thinking too much more about it. After a lot of effort by Kim and TJ to bring us together, Vicki emailed me. This eventually turned into text messages, which eventually became phone calls, and then video calls. After a few months digital communication, we finally met up again on Thanksgiving and immediately hit if off. That Thanksgiving weekend is when I figured out that I was going to marry Vicki. And so here I am.
